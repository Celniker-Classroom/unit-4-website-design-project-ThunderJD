Age/DOB: Born October 21, 1982(Bagan Serai, Perak, Malaysia)

Primary hand: Right handed,incredible speed and stamina

Nicknames: Dato, The marathon man

Rackets Used: Yonex Voltric Z-Force II, Yonex Astrox 77, Yonex Astrox 99

Key Achievements: 3x Olympic Silver Medalist (2008,2012,2016), 349 weeks ranked No 1.


The Dedicated Hero

Dato' Lee Chong Wei is a Malaysian sporting legend known for his incredible endurance, blinding speed, and an attacking style that forced opponents into mistakes. He held the World Number One ranking for a record 349 weeks, including a 199-week streak. Despite consistently placing second behind Lin Dan in major finals, his "never give up" attitude made him a beloved figure globally.

Interesting Facts & Legacy

  1. Lucky 11: Started playing Badminton at age 11 after his mother banned him from playing basketball due to the heat.
  2. Comeback Spirit: Showed immense mental fortitude, continuing to win tournaments even after facing career-threatening accidents and, later, nose cancer, which forced his retirment.
