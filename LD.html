1. Lin Dan(林丹)-"Super Dan"


Age/DOB: Born October 14, 1983(Longyan,Fujian, China)

Primary hand: Left handed attacker

Nicknames: Super Dan

Rackets Used: Yonex Voltric Z-Force II, Yonex Astrox 99

Key Achievements: 2x Olypmic Gold(2008,2015) 5x World Champion, 6x All England Champion

The Unstoppable Force

Lin Dan is widely regarded as the greatest badminton player of all time. He is the first and only player to complete the "Double Grand Slam" winning all nine major international titles twice.

Interesting Facts & Legacy

  1. Pianist to Prodigy: Parents wanted him to learn piano, but he chose badminton at age five.
  2. The Rivalry: His rivalry with Lee Chong Wei is considered the greatest in Badminton history.
